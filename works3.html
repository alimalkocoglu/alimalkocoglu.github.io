SHFT - Life Coaching Course Sales Page Design


Deliverables:

Research based persona development.
User interview, survey design and user testing.
Information architecture design and user flows.
Prototyping from low-fi to hi-fi design mockups.
html / css


The Product

A sales website and the funnel including drip campaign and other integrated marketing for The Catalyst Life Coaching Intensive, a 16-week introduction to the theory and practice of life coaching.

My Role

I joined the SHFT team at an early stage as UX and UI designer. I worked closely with the product manager and managed the design efforts for the entire product experience from scratch in addition to working with the marketing team with user acquisition efforts.

Challenge - Objective

Objective of homepage design was to get more qualified leads for the sales team and increase the conversion rates and automating the sales funnel. I developed a content strategy based on user insights inferred from the customer journey map.

Conversion rates was really low and this was because of the homepage that was not designed considering the sales funnel.

User Journey Map

In order to create a journey map that took into account the kind of information users need before signing up, my team and I interviewed users and gathered insights on what they considered as they contemplated signing up to SHFT. My main focus was on finding the information needs for pre and post purchase periods. I validated this information with the sales team by reviewing the most asked questions during the sales process.


Average Time to Purchase

I partnered with the marketing team to analyze the typical length for a visitor to become a paid customer for the SHFT life coaching course, and we found that it took an average of 6 months. This metric shaped my design principles - resulting in a sales funnel strategy that focused on different phases of user interactions with multiple touch points and content such as drip campaigns, webinars, e-books, and social media.

The typical length for a visitor to become a paid customer for the SHFT life coaching course and the result was more than a few months.


Content Strategy - Blueprint

After conducting user interviews, working on the empathy map and assessing the sales funnel. I provided the marketing and sales team with a blueprint of the content. I organized the information into three sections: “prior to course”, “during the course” and “post-course” phases.


Results

Redesiged homepage increased the conversion by 13.8% with the findings of user interviews and journey maps product team developed a drip campaign and a webinar.
