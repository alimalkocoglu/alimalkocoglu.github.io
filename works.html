The Product

The Catalyst Life Coaching Intensive is a 16-week introduction to the theory and practice of life coaching, as taught by masters and PhD-level coaches and instructors.

My Role

I joined the SHFT team at an early stage as UX and UI designer. I worked closely with the product manager and manage the design efforts for the entire product experience from scratch in addition to working with the marketing team with useracquisition efforts.

Challange - Objective

After increasing the conversion rates for different SHFT products, (which can be found in other work examples) The team and I shifted our focus to increase the retention rate.

Discovery-User Research

I was inspired by Facebook and Twitter design teams’ approach to finding what makes a user come back and repurchase. In order to deconstruct the path that leads to becoming a power user, I closely looked at SHFT’s existing power users behaviour: I examined which SHFT products they have purchased, and how they behave / interact with the others inside online group. I also analyzed SHFT’s most popular products (in this case online life coaching classes) to see if they exhibit correlations of user behavior.

There was a positive correlaction between engagement rates of power users and the frequency of their purchases.

I analyzed highly engaged users’ entry points to SHFT product ecosystem, the online courses that they participated in as well as the frequency of purchases and relevant demographic data.There was a positive correlation between engagement rates of power users and the timeline of their purchases. So we had to look at ways to increase engagement and incentivize purchases.


Reply / comment structure

Users frequently mentioned in their interviews that they had problems reading all the other member’s comments and the current structure structure distracts them from engaging with the main topic,posted by the life coach of the group.
