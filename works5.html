The Product

A market place for SHFT trained life coaches that enables them to market themselves with customizable profile pages and make sales.

My Role

I joined the SHFT team at an early stage as UX and UI designer. I worked closely with the product manager and manage the design efforts for the entire product experience from scratch in addition to working with the marketing team with useracquisition efforts.

Objective

To design a customizable SHFT Life Coach profile page template with an embedded checkout process.With this structure in place, the coaches would not need to take their services off platform by building their own websites, resulting in more commissions revenue for SHFT per transaction.

Profile pages also allowed SHFT to lay the groundwork for, and begin to generate revenue, via a client and coaching marketplace. Also eliminated the need for coaches to take their services off platform or to build their own websites.

Discovery-User Research

SHFT life coaches were not incredibly tech savvy, and building a website was a huge blocker in starting their online coaching business and finding clients. Coaches wanted their website to be customizable with individual pricing plans and testimonials. Coaches wanted clients to be able to learn about them via their bio and other uniqueness indicators.


Design Decision / Critique

After the initial sketching process I presented two designs to the product team and had an internal design critique session. Albeit the one with a bigger profile image was the winner because of the concerns about getting a high quality picture that will fit into most mobile and desktop use cases we decided to go with the smaller life coach image version.
