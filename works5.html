SHFT Life Coaching Course sales page design


Deliverables:

Research based persona development.
User interview, survey design and user testing.
Information architecture design and user flows.
Prototyping from low-fi to hi-fi design mockups.
html / css


The Product

A market place for SHFT trained life coaches that enables them to market themselves with customizable profile pages and make sales.

My Role

I joined the SHFT team at an early stage as UX and UI designer. I worked closely with the product manager and manage the design efforts for the entire product experience from scratch in addition to working with the marketing team with useracquisition efforts.

Objective

To design a customizable SHFT Life Coach profile page template with an embedded checkout process.With this structure in place, the coaches would not need to take their services off platform by building their own websites, resulting in more commissions revenue for SHFT per transaction.

Discovery-User Research

After interviewing the SHFT life coaches, I found that they were not tech savvy, and building a website was a blocker in starting their online coaching business. Coaches wanted their website to be customizable with individual pricing plans and testimonials. Coaches also wanted clients to be able to learn about them via bio and other uniqueness indicators.


Design Decision / Critique

After the initial sketching process I presented two profile page designs to the product team and we had an internal critique session. We discussed the aesthetic and functional features of the page; for instance, in deciding on the visual of the coach in the profile page, the team initially preferred a bigger profile image, but because of the concerns that a bigger image would require our coaches to provide a high quality photograph - something that not every couch would necessarily have - we decided to go with a smaller, less technically demanding profile photo.



Pricing Page

After conducting interviews with potential life coaching clients, I highlighted the money back guarantee to address their concerns about investing in a new type of life coaching product. Also, the clients indicated that they needed guidance in order to determine how many sessions they should purchase - I addressed this by coming up with pricing plan titles to indicate the expected outcome for a potential client, and included testimonials from previous SHFT life coaching users.
