The Product

Offmarket.com is a broker-free platform for buying and selling commercial real estate with end-to-end transaction features that include a market place, marketing tools, deal boards and electronic confidentiality agreements.

My Role

I joined Offmarket.com as a UX designer and researcher. I worked closely with the product manager and founders to improve the existing designs, user flows and product validation processes.

Discovery-User Research

I designed and conducted user interviews to understand the motivations and key information pieces for the target personas (real estate professionals). My research focused on the interactions between real estate professionals and brokers. Selling without a broker is a more complex process than buying, so my goal was to devise an easier design flow for the sellers to quickly increase on-site inventory.


User Flow

I analyzed the user sign-up flow and reduced any superfluous steps that slowed sellers from listing a property for sale. This streamlined the overall process for the potential seller, our focal party in the two-sided real estate marketplace. I devised a contextual user sign-up flow that differed for buyers and sellers and aligned with the business goals of our product.


A/B Testing

To validate the new designs I devised through user interviews and flow analysis, I set up an A/B test for the contextual sign up flow. I ran tests with different CTA’s, as well as four different value props.
