The Product

The Catalyst Life Coaching Intensive is a 16-week introduction to the theory and practice of life coaching, as taught by masters and PhD-level coaches and instructors.

My Role

I joined the SHFT team at an early stage as UX and UI designer. I worked closely with the product manager and manage the design efforts for the entire product experience from scratch in addition to working with the marketing team with useracquisition efforts.

Challange - Objective

Objective of homepage design was to get more qualified leads for the sales team and increase the conversion rates and automating the process. I created a content strategy based on user insights projected on customer journey map.

Conversion rates was really low and this was because of the homepage that was not designed considering the sales funnel.

User Journey Map

In order to create a journey map focused on different information needs of potential users me and my team interviewed a few users and gathered insights on what they looked for before signing up. My main focus was finding the information needs for pre and post purchase periods. I validated this information with the interviews I conducted with the sales team by gathering the most asked questions during the sales process.


Average Time to Purchase

I partnered with the marketing team to analyse the typical length for a visitor to become a paid customer for the SHFT life coaching course and the result was more than a few months. It showed that a customer interacts with the product at multiple touch points with the product with different information needs. This metric shaped my design principles different than a regular immediate sales focused ecommerce approach.

Content Strategy - Blueprint

After conducting the user interviews, working on the emphaty map and asseing the sales funnel I provided the marketing & sales team with the blueprint of the content headlines and reference points.


Results

Redesiged homepage increased the conversion by %13.8 With the findings of user interviews and journey maps product team developed a drip campaing and a webinar.
