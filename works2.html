The Product

The Catalyst Life Coaching Intensive is a 16-week introduction to the theory and practice of life coaching, as taught by masters and PhD-level coaches and instructors.

My Role

I joined the SHFT team at an early stage as UX and UI designer. I worked closely with the product manager and managed the design efforts for the entire product experience from scratch in addition to working with the marketing team with user acquisition efforts.

Challenge - Objective

SHFT is a multifaceted product providing online group coaching sessions, users had difficulty understanding the scope and breadth of the product. These clarification issues led to high bounce rates that resulted in lower conversion rates.

Discovery - User Research

After conducting user interviews, I found that users who visited the home page did not understand the product from our description, additionally they misunderstood how the free trial version worked as well as how much time, money and commitment was needed from them to participate.

Using too much insider product terminology confused users.


Card Sorting - Making sense of categories

I conducted open and closed card sorting exercises in order to define the categories for SHFT products and design the information architechure for the profile creation page after sign-up process.

Finetune the copy - User Testing

I redesigned a new draft of the homepage based on user feedback, I decided to test the new homepage with remote users, who were unfamiliar with the product, before spending any additional marketing money.

I devised a clarity score that addressed the confusion points from initial user tests and worked closely with the marketing team to fine tune the copy based on our findings during user testing. I pointed the testing results after every user test and calculated an average clarity score for every version. After 36 remote users tests and 5 iterations I was ready to put up the page in front of real users.

Results

Increased understanding of the multifacted product ecosystem from existing users as well as a substantial overall increase in user acquisition from 4% conversion to 15% conversion for cold traffic.
